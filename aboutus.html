OIC FOUNDATION
Is a registered IT firm in Nigeria that commenced operation since July 2016 and duly registered under Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission on February 2019. We equip people with digital skills and soft skills like goal setting, team work, time management thereby mobilizing the skills to solve immediate and future challenges of our society in various sectors. We have a tri-focal point that guides us; We Train, Develop and Mentor. We are passionate about Africans and their exposure and access to technology. We have hosted several activities where young Africans and teachers were trained under the sponsorship of stakeholders and paid programmes. We have series of experience in training children, youths and adults. Our previous programs can be easily accessed via our website and social media.

OUR TEAM

At OIC Foundation, we are passionate about young Africans and their exposure and access to technology. We are a team of experienced and professional tech educators providing quality training on digital skills with our state-of-the-art tools. We have hosted several activities where young and adults Nigerians were trained for both sponsorship and paid programs. We have series of experience in training children and women. Our organization has organized several programs in collaboration with Ministry of Education and other mentioned organizations and the program were hundred percent successful. Our previous programs can view through our website and Social media.

MISSION
